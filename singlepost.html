Summer Camp Registration Now Open

JANUARY 10, 2026

We're excited to announce that registration is now open for the North City Bruins Summer Basketball Camp! This intensive week-long program is designed to take your game to the next level.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Our summer camps feature full-day training sessions from 9 AM to 4 PM, Monday through Friday. Each day includes skill development drills, competitive scrimmages, position-specific training, and guest coaching from high school and college coaches. We also incorporate team-building activities and basketball IQ sessions to develop well-rounded players.

PROGRAM DETAILS

Camps are available for ages 8-17, divided into age-appropriate groups to ensure optimal learning and competition. We offer multiple week-long sessions throughout July and August. Each camp is limited to 50 participants to maintain a low coach-to-player ratio and provide personalized attention.

REGISTRATION INFORMATION

Early bird pricing is available until March 1st. Spaces fill up quickly, so we encourage families to register early. Financial assistance is available for qualifying families - please contact us for more information. All camp participants receive a North City Bruins training jersey and basketball.

CONTACT US

For more information or to register, visit our contact page or email us directly. Don't miss this opportunity to train with the best youth basketball program in Toronto!
