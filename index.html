FAQ

The basics for parents and players.

How are teams formed?
Teams are balanced by age group and skill level based on evaluations and prior experience. We prioritize a fun, fair, and developmental environment.
What equipment do players need?
Indoor shoes, a water bottle, and a ball for practice. Jerseys are provided. Optional: mouthguard and knee pads.
Do you offer financial assistance?
Yes. Limited scholarships are available. Contact us to request a confidential application.
