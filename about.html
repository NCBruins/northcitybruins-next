About North City Bruins

Founded in Toronto, Ontario, North City Bruins is dedicated to fostering a love of basketball among youth of all ages. Our programs emphasize skill development, teamwork, sportsmanship, and personal growth. We believe in creating a supportive and inclusive environment where every player can thrive on and off the court.

Our Mission

To inspire the next generation of athletes by providing high-quality coaching, competitive opportunities, and a safe and positive community. We aim to help each player reach their full potential, whether they are new to the game or striving for elite competition.

Programs Offered

  • Learn-to-Play: Introductory skills and drills for ages 6–9.
  • Recreational Leagues: Fun and inclusive play for ages 9–13.
  • Travel Teams: Competitive play and tournaments for ages 12–17.
  • Clinics & Camps: Seasonal clinics to focus on fundamentals.

Coaching Staff

Our coaches are certified, experienced, and passionate about youth development. Many have collegiate or professional playing experience and all are committed to the Bruins' core values of integrity, commitment, and fun.
