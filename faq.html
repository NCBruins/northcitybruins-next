Frequently Asked Questions

General

When does the season start?

Our winter season typically begins in late January and runs through early April. Check the Home page or contact us for the exact dates.

What age groups do you serve?

We offer programs for boys and girls ages 6–17. Teams are grouped by age and skill level to ensure a fun and competitive experience for everyone.

How do I register?

Registration is completed online via our registration portal. Please visit the Contact page to request registration information or click the Register link when it becomes available each season.

Equipment & Uniforms

What equipment do players need?

Players should bring proper basketball shoes, athletic wear and a water bottle. Team jerseys will be provided at the start of the season and must be returned afterwards.

Can I keep my jersey?

Jerseys and practice uniforms are the property of North City Bruins and should be returned at the end of the season. Players will receive a Bruins t‑shirt to keep.

Volunteering

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers for coaching, scorekeeping and fundraising events. Visit our Contact page to let us know you’re interested and we’ll get in touch.
